Welcome to Cebu's Best Staycation Rentals

Cebu's Best Staycation Rentals is a family-owned Airbnb and rental business based in Cebu. Our flagship service is an Airbnb rental located at 38th Park Avenue, nestled in the heart of IT Park Cebu.

We pride ourselves on providing comfortable and convenient accommodations for travelers and tourists visiting Cebu. Our goal is to ensure that our guests have a memorable stay and experience the best of what Cebu has to offer.
